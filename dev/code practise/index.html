Soupy the Dragon

Who I Iz

I am the big dragon. The big smelly, soupy, slimy dragon. You want soup? I've got soup. Flavour is my middle name! "What flavours do you have?", I hear ye backon, well my friends... I have all of them! The life of a soup dragon is a simple life. In the foothills of the mountains here, I roam the forest floor, keenly searching for the finest ingredients. Anything goes in my soup, from mushrooms (yes, the magic ones), to pork chops, to the unsuspecting human kidney. It has a particular je ne sais quoi which I simply cannot resist, you see...

Regardless, in the current uninspiring socioeconomic climate which plagues this kingdom today, I must offer my services. If you are a culinary establishment in need of an exceptional soup chef, I would be more than happy to talk to you. Please hire me.
